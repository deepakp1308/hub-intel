# L2C Strategic Context for HUB-INTEL

## What is L2C?
Lead-to-Cash is Mailchimp/Intuit's #1 strategic bet for FY27, targeting $300M revenue over 3 years. HubSpot is the primary competitive threat. L2C covers: generating leads, managing leads, closing leads, managing projects, getting paid.

## L2C Components
- **Marketing App:** Email campaigns, automations, lead ads (Meta, Google, LinkedIn), Brand Kit, omnichannel builder
- **Customer Hub App:** CRM, proposals, contracts, e-signatures, pipeline, Kanban, customer AI agent
- **Sales & Get Paid App:** Invoicing, payments (Adyen for intl), project management, time tracking

## Target ICP
- Professional Services (construction, consultants, agencies, field services)
- 1-10 employees, $0.5-2.5M revenue
- International: Germany, Spain, Italy, Netherlands + US

## Competitive Moat — Counter Positioning
Intuit owns BOTH marketing engagement data (Mailchimp) AND financial records (QuickBooks). No competitor has both. This enables **ledger-anchored attribution** — campaign-to-invoice-paid ROI verified against the actual financial ledger. CRM incumbents (HubSpot, Salesforce, Freshworks) would need to build or acquire a financial management system to match. They are structurally disincentivized from entering our territory.

## What We Uniquely Deliver (HubSpot Cannot)
- Proposal performance analytics (template conversion, time-to-sign, abandonment)
- Contract-to-cash tracking (signed → invoiced → paid)
- Payment/DSO analytics (DSO velocity, late payers, aging, revenue waterfall)
- Project profitability (budget vs actual, margin trend)
- End-to-end L2C funnel (7 stages: campaign → lead → opportunity → proposal → invoice → payment)
- Customer-level LTV prediction
- Ledger-verified revenue (QB general ledger, not CRM deal estimates)

## HubSpot Competitive Gaps
- Gates attribution behind Enterprise tier ($3,600+/yr) — we include in all tiers
- Stops at deal close — no proposal performance, contract-to-cash, payment/DSO
- No ledger-verified revenue — uses CRM deal amounts only
- No project profitability tracking
- No customer-level LTV prediction
- Basic meeting analytics (counts only)
